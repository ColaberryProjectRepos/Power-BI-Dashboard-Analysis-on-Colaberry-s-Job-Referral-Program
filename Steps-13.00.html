1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

?INTRODUCTION:

As an ever-growing organization, Colaberry needs to keep an eye on their ultimate goal - Job Placement for each and every student.  It is important for them to know what the job market looks like, to ensure the best referrals for its graduates whether by location or from month to month. 

In today's project, we will be using Microsoft Power BI, to import data from various sources, as well as extracting from an OLEDB  source to create our data sets and reports. We will learn how DAX calculations work, create calculated columns and measures and design simple and complex visualizations.

OVERVIEW:

Colaberry uses the Job referral Program to account for and track job opportunities available to its students. The data retrieved from our OLEDB source gives us an opportunity to dissect and analyze the trends surrounding the job market to better serve the students/applicants from Colaberry. Below is a snapshot of each page of our report and a brief explanation of the visualizations.


HOMEPAGE: 

The Home Page of our Report gives us a general overview. On this Page, we have general KPI's that will be further discussed in the consequent steps. These KPI's include, the Average number of Open Job Referrals, Total Number of Job Referrals, and the Trending Job Tags by month.


OPEN JOB DETAILS:

In Colaberry, Jobs posted on the timeline, can either be labeled "New", "Open" or "Closed". However, on this page, we will be analyzing ONLY OPEN Job Referrals. Our dashboard breaks it down into the Job Titles, Company Name, Number of submissions, and aggregations such as the Count of companies by JobTags. It is interesting to note that this entire page can be filtered by Job Tag of your choice.


SPECIFIC JOB DETAILS:

The previous page ("Open Job Details") drills through to This Page and provides a detailed account of the previously selected Open Job. This includes the Job Description, Company Name, Job Status, Active Status reason, Top Job tag, just to name a few.


SALES REP OF THE MONTH:  

Using a Funnel Chart to show the Sales Rep with the highest Jobs referred with the ability to filter by Year or by Job Referral Status Description or by Job Tag.
