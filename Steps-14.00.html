2. DATA MODEL

Data Model for Job Referral Program

In SQL Server, we created a View by using join statements to bring several tables together to increase query performance. The two tables JobReferral_VW and CB_MKTG_JobTags were retrieved by connecting to SQL Server.



JOB REFERRAL_VW:

This Job Referral View is made up of a combination of several tables and includes data like the Referred Company Names, Job Titles, Date of Entry, Number of Submissions, Recruiter Info, Colaberry Sales Rep Info, Job Status and so much more. 

JOB TAGS

Our Job Tags Table contains the Job IDs and their corresponding JobTags.
